How I create a marketing plan

5 steps for a successful strategy:
  • analyze your business and its position on the web (SWOT analysis);
  • identify your competitors and understand how they behave;
  • define your target audience and specific, measurable, attainable, realistic and timely (SMART) goals to reach;
  • plan a strategy by choosing the channels to use to promote your products/services
  • create contents and value for your business and measure the feedback after a while to see if the strategy works
